Get involved

There's a place for you here

Organizer, volunteer, ally, survivor, friend: however you arrive, you're welcome. Here's how to plug in.

Join and stay connected

Join the club

Fill out our interest form and we'll reach out with meeting times and what's coming up. Open to all CU Boulder students. No experience needed, just care.

Get the newsletter

Event announcements, meeting reminders, and ways to help, straight to your inbox. No spam, ever.

Contact us

Send us a message

Questions, ideas, collaborations, or anything else, we'd love to hear from you. Messages go straight to sacsa@colorado.edu.

This form is for general questions and club business, and it is not monitored around the clock and is not a crisis line. If you need support now, please see our resources page.

Other ways to help

Show up, speak up, share

Come to an event and bring a friend. Volunteer at our table. Help paint signs before Take Back the Night. Tell someone about the resources on this site. You never know who needs them.

Every bit of it moves campus culture in the right direction.
